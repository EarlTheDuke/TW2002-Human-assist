Close the Observation/format_observation gap for LLM agents

The audit documented in docs/AGENT_TURN_ANATOMY.md found that the
SYSTEM_PROMPT instructed agents to reason about trade_log,
owned_planets, net_worth, structured goals, and survival fields, but
format_observation was stripping all of those before the user message
went out. Agents were literally being told "read your trade_log" with
no trade_log in their input.

format_observation now ships everything the prompt references:

  top-level: goals, trade_log (last 5), owned_planets, alliances, corp
  self.*:    net_worth, alignment_label, rank, experience,
             alive, deaths, max_deaths

User-message payload grew 5095 -> 6055 chars (+19 percent, approx
+240 tokens per turn). Trivial cost relative to the 400k token/min
limit and the 3500-token system prompt. The three remaining un-sent
Observation fields (finished, limpets_owned, probe_log) are genuinely
niche.

Four regression tests locked this in under TestPhaseGObservationSurface
so no future refactor can silently drop these fields again. Doc section
4 and section 8 updated to reflect the fix; artifacts in
docs/agent_turn/ regenerated.
